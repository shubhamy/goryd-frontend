About Us

We are a bunch of engineers with corporate experience in software engineering and business analytics. We are building Goryd with a vision to solve the problem of car renting in India. With the use of technology, we aim to build a trusted community of travelers and car owners who believe in sharing.

Our platform will enable car owners to earn money even while their cars are idle. This can be done by registering their cars free of cost on our platform. When a tentative booking is made, car owners have a choice of approving or rejecting the booking. This approval can be given after establishing contact with customers, even meeting in person over a cup of coffee.

Our technology will make sure that your car is safe with us.
